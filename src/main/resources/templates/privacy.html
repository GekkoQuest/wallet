Privacy Notice

Your privacy and security are our top priorities

🔒 What We Collect

  • Email addresses: Used only for authentication and login verification
  • Encrypted password data: Your passwords are encrypted client-side before reaching our servers
  • Session data: Temporary authentication tokens for secure login sessions

🚫 What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ No cookies or tracking: We don't use any tracking technologies
  • ❌ No data selling: Your data is never sold, shared, or monetized
  • ❌ No plain text passwords: We never see your actual passwords
  • ❌ No third-party sharing: Your data stays with us exclusively
  • ❌ No advertising: We don't serve ads or track user behavior

🔐 Security Measures

  • 🛡️ Client-side encryption: All passwords are encrypted in your browser before transmission
  • 🔑 Zero-knowledge architecture: We cannot decrypt your passwords even if we wanted to
  • 🚀 HTTPS everywhere: All connections are secured with industry-standard encryption
  • ⏰ Session management: Automatic logout and secure session handling
  • 🔍 No logging: We don't log your password access or usage patterns

📋 Important Notes

This is a demonstration project: While we implement strong security practices, this application is designed for educational and portfolio purposes.

Data retention: We store your encrypted data until you choose to delete your account. You can request data deletion at any time.

Contact: If you have questions about privacy or security, please contact us through the appropriate channels.
